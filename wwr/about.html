History

Over the years, we have joyously served over 10,000 customers, each leaving with a story to tell and a heart full of exhilaration. In 2023 alone, we had the privilege of bringing the thrill of rafting to more than a thousand adventurers, sharing the beauty of nature and the excitement of the rapids. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship has made us a trusted name in the industry, ensuring that every trip down the river is not just an adventure, but a memory to cherish for a lifetime.

Also for over 15 years, our rafting company has been dedicated to teaching the art of rafting to teenagers, instilling in them a love for adventure and nature. Our history is rich with stories of young hearts pounding with excitement as they navigate the rapids, learning not just the skills of rafting, but also the values of teamwork, courage, and respect for the environment. We take pride in our role as educators, shaping the next generation of rafters and nature lovers.
